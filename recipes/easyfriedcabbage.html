Easy Fried Cabbage

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons grapeseed oil
  • 1 medium yellow onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons Cajun seasoning, or to taste, divided
  • 0.5 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 0.5 medium green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 medium head cabbage, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon butter, or more to taste (Optional)
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat grapseed oil in a large cast iron skillet over medium heat. Add onions and cook until softened, about 3 minutes. Add garlic, stir, and cook until fragrant, 1 to 2 minutes. Season with 1 teaspoon Cajun seasoning and stir to combine.
  2. Mix in red and green bell peppersl cook for 1 to 2 minutes. Add cabbage, remaining 1 teaspoon Cajun seasoning, and stir to combine. Add butter and stir until melted. Simmer cabbage until it reaches desired softness, about 15 minutes. Season with salt and pepper

Kafta (BBQ)

Description

Have you ever heard of Lebanon? The Lebanese people went though a horrible civil war only decades ago, all stemming from governmental instability that's rooted in the second world war after they gained their independence from France.

This dish is a delicious Lebanese classic that will surely remind you of the Sabra and Shatila massacres! These weird meatballs will surely fill you up!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds lean ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, grated
  • 0.5 cup finely chopped fresh parsley
  • 0.25 teaspoon ground allspice
  • 1 teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • 0.25 teaspoon black pepper
  • 6 wooden or metal skewers

Steps

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat. If using wooden skewers, soak in water.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together the ground beef, onion, parsley, cayenne, allspice, salt and pepper until evenly blended. Divide into 7 portions, and press around one end of the skewers to form a log shape approximately 1 inch thick and 6 inches long.
  3. Grill for 10 to 15 minutes, turning occasionally, until meat is no longer pink

Mediterranean Greek Salad

Desciption

This delicious salad is the root of the strength of Rome. Through years of conquest, Julius Ceaser and his little disciples traveled around Greece and Rome to find the perfect ingredients to put in a bucket and mix together. There, they found the ingredients to a poop-colors salad.

Ingredients

  • 3 cucumbers, seeded and slices
  • 1.5 cups crumbled feta cheese
  • 1 cup black olives, pitted and sliced
  • 3 cups diced roma tomatoes
  • .333 cup diced oil packed sun-dried tomatoes, drained, oil reserved
  • 0.5 red onion, sliced

Steps

  1. In a large salad bowl, toss together the cucumbers, feta cheese, alives, roma tomatoes, sun-dried tomatoes, 2 tablespoons reserved sun-dried tomato oil, and red onion, Chill until serving.
